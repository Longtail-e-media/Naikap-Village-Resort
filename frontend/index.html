Unwind & Relax at Naikap Village Resort

Welcome to Naikap Village Resort, one of the best luxurious newly built resort located at Purano Naikap, Kathmandu, 15 minutes drive from Kalanki Chowk. It's a hub for entertainment destinations and it quickly connects to the perfect combination of business and pleasure needs. We embody experience, expertise and excellence as our company is managed by group of tourism professionals.
With beautiful rooms, all the modern amenities,a restaurant, terrace dining experience, beautiful garden, we further provide extensive conference and banquet spaces to provide an ideal venue for larger events and meetings.
